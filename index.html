About
This pack adds progression and realistic features to minecraft to make it much more difficult, it also makes minecraft look and feel much more real by adding dynamic villages, more realistic chests, and furnaces, and by adding more realistic villagers.

 

Note
Several crafting recipes have been changed in this pack, make sure to use JEI to look up the recipes in the pack
